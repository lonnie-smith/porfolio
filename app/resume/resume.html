Product development lead, Educational Testing Service. 2010–2013

I led a team that designed and built 21 engineering and technology problem-solving tasks on contract for the U.S. Department of Education. You can see examples of these tasks here.

  • I managed the work of 7 subcontractors, a content development staff, and a small group of designers. We developed ~500 minutes of interactive assessment content.
  • I wore a lot of hats on this project. In addition to general product-manager duties, I worked closely with the client to ensure their vision for the assessment would be realized, designed user interfaces, worked with cognitive psychologists to define measurement targets for the assessment, and trained staff that scored user responses. I also shepherded content through a multi-stage expert review process, and oversaw usability/user testing activities.
  • The product launched on time, and tens of thousands of students took this assessment in the winter of 2013 and again in 2014. Students and teachers had overwhelmingly positivie reactions to the assessment, and our content has subsequently awarded us contracts for developing similar digital content.
